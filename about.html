Thanks for stopping by! My name is Abigail, but I typically go by Abby (and some of my friends call me dabby). I am originally from California, but I recently moved up to Seattle for Ada. I graduated from University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) with a B.S in Molecular, Cell, Developmental Biology with the original goal of becoming a physician.

Throughout my pre-med journey, I had the opportunity to work on a project focused on a passive mobile self-tracking app for veterans with severe mental illness(SMI). During that time, I developed a greater interest in the reach and scalability of tech-based approaches and was motivated to learn more about coding. Fast-forward about a year, and here I am now!
